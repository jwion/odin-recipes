Cheddar Bacon Cheeseburgers

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Hamburger
  • 4 Brioche Hamburger Buns
  • Toppings (lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayo, etc.)
  • 1 16 oz. bag sliced cheddar cheese
  • 16 oz. of bacon

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger.
  2. Boil lasagna until al dente.
  3. In skillet, combine hamburger and sauce. Let simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Layer lasagna, hamburger mixture, and cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
