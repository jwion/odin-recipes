Grilled Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of Honey Oat bread
  • 2 tbs. butter
  • 2 tbs. mayo
  • 1 32 oz. bag of cheddar cheese
  • 1 32 oz. container of Pre-Cooked Ham slices

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger.
  2. Boil lasagna until al dente.
  3. In skillet, combine hamburger and sauce. Let simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Layer lasagna, hamburger mixture, and cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
